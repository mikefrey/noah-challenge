HOW TO PLAY

Note: This is a closed game. If you arrived here without an invite please refrain from signing up.
  1. Enter the Game
    • Click the “Get My Ballot” link to get started.
    • If you have not yet signed up for this year, you will be asked for you name, email, password and the secret invite code.
    • If you have already created your account you can simply log in to see your ballot.
    • If you have trouble with this step, please contact us.
  2. Make Your Picks
    • In each of the 24 categories assign the 10 available points (20 for Best Picture) any way you wish.
    • As you assign points to nominees the number of points remaining will be reflected below the category title (see below).
    • Be sure you use ALL of the available points (see below).
    • You will be awarded with the number of points you assigned to the winner of each category as they are announced.
    • Note: in the event of a tie in any category, points will be awarded for all winners.
    • Picks will lock at Noon on Sunday, February 26. You may change your picks at any time before the deadline.
  3. Pay Your Entry
    • Payment is by PayPal only. Please contact us for a PayPal link.
    • Entry is due by Noon on Sunday, February 26.
    • Winnings are awarded as follows:
      2/3 of the combined entry fees will go to the ballot with the highest score with the remaining 1/3 going to the second-highest score.
      In the event of a tie for first place, the combined entry fees will be equally divided between the tied entrants.
  4. Watch the Show
    • Tune in to The Oscars on Sunday, February 26 (6:00pm CST on ABC) and track your results during the show.
    • Scores will be updated after most categories and can be seen on the Results page.
