HOW TO PLAY

Note: This is a closed game. If you arrived here without an invite please refrain from signing up.
  1. Create Your Ballot
    • To get a ballot you need an account.
    • Follow the instructions on the home page to sign up for an account.
    • If you have already created your account you can simply log in to see your ballot.
    • If you have trouble with this step, please contact us.
  2. Make Your Picks
    • In each of the 21 categories assign the 10 available points (20 for Best Picture) any way you wish.
    • As you assign points to nominees the number of points remaining will be reflected to the right of the category title (see below)
    • Be sure you use ALL of the available points (see below).
    • You will be awarded with the number of points you assigned to the winners as they are announced.
    • Note: in the event of a tie in any category, points will be awarded for all winners.
    • Picks will lock at Noon on Sunday, February 22. You may change you picks at any time before the deadline.
  3. Pay Your Entry
    • Payment is by PayPal only. Please contact us for a PayPal link.
    • Entry is due by Noon on Sunday, February 22.
    • Winnings are awarded as follows:
      2/3 of the combined entry fees will go to the ballot with the highest score with the remaining 1/3 going to the second-highest score.
  4. Watch the Show
    • Tune in to The Oscars on Sunday, February 22 (6:00pm ABC) and track your results during the show.
    • Scores will be updated after most categories and can be seen on the Results page.
